About Best Wish

We at Best Wish think that birthdays are important and a great opportunity to bring people closer together. Your loved one deserve a happy birthday wish from you. This should not take so much time and mental energy from you. Best Wish have a solution for you! Scroll down to learn more about our free offer!

Reminder

Get notified about your friends upcoming birthday. Just like Facebook but instead 7 days before in case that you want to order a present on the internet or plan a party for your lucky friend.

Birthday Card

We will send your friend list a birthday card on their birthday from you! (Your name will be on the card). You can add or remove friends from your Best Wish friend list at anytime.

Facebook (Cooming soon)

Add your friends to your Best Wish friends list directly from your Facebook contact list instead of adding them manually. This function is only available on our upcoming App.

Free

Best Wish is completely free. No one time purchase, No subscription fee, we don't even charge you for the birthday cards we send to your friend from you. Sign up now!

Customize

You are able to edit your friends list at anytime. You can add more people to your list or remove. You can also at anytime edit your friends list information like , address, contact information and etc.

Gift sugestion (Cooming soon)

Get tips what to buy for your friend's birthday. All suggestions are based on your friend's interest. Order the same or use it to get some idea what to get for your friend

How to use Best Wish?

Sign-Up

Sign-up by clicking on sign-up button and fill in your information to creat an account. We are following the GDPR rules in EU and your information is safe with us. We will not share your data with third party.

Add friends

Add people you want to be reminds to your Best Wish list. You can add, remove or change your friend list at anytime. The auto sync function does not work right now and you need to enter each friend manually.

Reminder

We will send you an email 7 days before your friend's birthday with some gift suggestions and tips. We also letting you know that we are going to send your friend a birthday card with your name on it.

Update

To make this process easier for us so we can provide the best service to you please update your profile manually or contact us if any of yours or your friend's information has changed.

Best Wish is a start up, we need your help to improve our service. Please contact us for feedbacks on any of our social media or mail.
